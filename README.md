How do I approach designing software?
  * What new design skills has your work on the project helped you to craft?
     * This project has enabled me to explore the elements related to lighting, specifically the diffuse,
       ambient and specular lighting and how placing them properly can add dimension and realism to any
       3D scene.
  * What design process did you follow for your project work?
    * Throughout this project, I took an iterative approach with designing this 3D scene. I took one of
      the more complex compound objects for this scene and made that a priority and when upon completion,
      I moved onto other elements of the scene so I could spend more time on the easier objects, after I
      had gotten experience with OpenGL.
  * How could tactics from your design approach be applied in future work?
    * The tactics that I employed stem from the Agile methodology with the iterative but continuous
      development approach, where I chose one object and worked it until I was satisfied with the results
      then moved onto the next object. Also, by starting with the most complex object, I was able to spend
      some time researching into methods that might improve other aspects of the scene, which gave me ample
      time to pivot in case I needed to change direction or design.
    
How do I approach developing programs?
  * What new development strategies did you use while working on your 3D scene?
    * I'm not sure if I'd call it a new development strategy but I did delve into the glsl shader files even

  * How did iteration factor into your development?
    * Iteration was very important to this development, because there are a lot of elements that must come
      together and coalesce into one cohesive scene. Every week brought new challenges and more reading,
      from adding lights, camera controls and even textures to the models. There was no way to start and finish
      a complex project like this in one sitting.
  * How has your approach to developing code evolved throughout the milestones, which led you to the project’s completion?
    * My approach to developing code was minimal as I have been developing various code for years now, but this has been
      the first time I have ever physically changed a glsl shader file, which is impressive to me, considering where I
      started out.
